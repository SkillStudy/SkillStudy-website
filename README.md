# SkillStudy

**Empowering Rural India Through Digital Literacy and Social Innovation**

SkillStudy is a mission-driven platform dedicated to equipping rural youth with modern technical skills and digital literacy. Our objective is to transform technology into an accessible medium for social change, serving as a bridge for individuals seeking to join the digital mainstream.

### Our Core Initiatives

*   **Digital Literacy:** Providing foundational computer education and internet safety training.
*   **Skill Development:** Empowering rural youth to achieve self-reliance through practical coding and digital tool proficiency.
*   **Community Support:** Facilitating direct access to essential government digital services and educational resources.

### Technologies & Tools

We utilize a modern, lightweight technology stack to build accessible educational tools and interfaces:
*   HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript for minimalist, high-performance web development.
*   Python and PHP for robust backend utility applications.
*   AI-powered educational assistants and automated content delivery integrations (Gemini API).
